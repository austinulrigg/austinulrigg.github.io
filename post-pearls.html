Pearls in Graph Theory: Solutions

Download the guide (PDF) →

A worked solution guide to selected sections (10.1, 10.2, and 10.3) of Pearls in Graph Theory by Hartsfield and Ringel. These sections sit in the part of the book on graph embeddings and genus, so the problems are about drawing graphs on surfaces without crossings.

This guide is meant to supplement your learning by providing clarifications and problem-solving techniques. Please use it responsibly: don't copy the solutions, but use them to check your work and deepen your understanding. Engaging with the material independently is essential for genuine learning and academic integrity.

You can read the full set of worked solutions in the PDF.
