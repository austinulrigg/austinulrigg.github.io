Expository paper · MATH 336

Maximum Cut and the Goemans–Williamson Algorithm

An expository paper introducing the Maximum Cut problem — one of the classic NP-hard problems in combinatorial optimization — and the Goemans–Williamson algorithm, a landmark approximation algorithm that uses semidefinite programming and randomized rounding to find provably near-optimal cuts.
