Math Tutoring

I tutor advanced high school and undergraduate math, one-on-one. The goal is simple: help you understand what’s going on, get unstuck, and feel more confident working through problems on your own.

I cover the UW calculus sequence, linear algebra, differential equations, and proof-based courses, plus higher topics like abstract algebra, topology, and graph theory by request. I finished my math degree at UW and have tutored for about five years.

Sessions are online or in person near UW, and rates run $30 to $50 an hour depending on the course. If money’s tight, reach out anyway and we’ll figure something out.
