Research

My research interests are in topological graph theory — especially graph embeddings, orientable genus, rotation systems, and forbidden minors. Each project below has its own page with a summary, the full document, and links.

Paper · with A. Metzger
An Efficient Genus Algorithm Based on Graph Rotations
PAGE: a rotation-system algorithm that computes the orientable genus of any finite connected graph. We used it to pin down the genus of the (3,12)-cage.
