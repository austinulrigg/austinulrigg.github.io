My research interests are in topological graph theory, especially graph embeddings, orientable genus, rotation systems, and forbidden minors.

## Publications and Research Writing

### An Efficient Genus Algorithm Based on Graph Rotations

With Alexander Metzger, I co-authored a paper on an algorithm that determines the orientable genus of a finite connected graph using graph rotations.

- [Read the project page](/posts/2024/11/an-efficient-genus-algorithm/)
- [View the paper on arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.07347)

### Graph Genera and Minors

My undergraduate honors thesis at the University of Washington studies graph embeddings on orientable surfaces, rotation systems, graph genera, and forbidden minors. The thesis was supervised by Francois Clement.

- [Read the thesis page](/posts/2025/12/2025-12-06-Graph-Genera-And-Minors/)
- [Download the thesis PDF](/files/Ulrigg_Honors_Thesis_2025.pdf)
- [View the UW ResearchWorks record](https://digital.lib.washington.edu/researchworks/items/63560af1-21d6-4068-a658-5a164b3f84f2)
